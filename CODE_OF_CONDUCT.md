# Code of Conduct

Welcome to Entprep! 👋  
We’re building a community that’s open, respectful, and supportive for everyone. This Code of Conduct exists to ensure a positive experience for all contributors.

---

## 🌟 Our Commitment

We are committed to providing a friendly, safe, and welcoming environment for all — regardless of background, gender identity, experience level, ethnicity, ability, or personal beliefs.

---

## ✅ What We Expect from Everyone

Be kind. Be respectful. Assume good intentions.

### Do:
- Be welcoming and inclusive.
- Respect differing viewpoints and experiences.
- Give constructive feedback, kindly.
- Ask for help when needed — we're all learning!

### Don’t:
- Use harmful or discriminatory language.
- Troll, flame, or harass others.
- Dismiss others' contributions or ideas rudely.
- Share private info of others without consent.

---

## 🚫 Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or discriminatory jokes
- Sexist, racist, or offensive comments
- Unwanted sexual attention or advances
- Repeatedly interrupting or disrupting discussions
- Posting spam or irrelevant content

---

## 🛠️ Reporting Problems

If you experience or see behavior that goes against this Code of Conduct, **please let us know** so we can help. You can:

- Open an issue on GitHub (if safe to do so), or  
- Contact a project maintainer privately:
  - **Maintainer**: [@roushnisareen](https://github.com/roushnisareen)

All reports will be handled respectfully and confidentially.

---

## 📘 Consequences

Breaking this Code may result in:

1. A friendly warning
2. Temporary ban from participation
3. Permanent removal from the project

The project maintainers have the right to take any action they think is necessary to protect the community.

---

## 🤝 Let’s Build Together

Open-source is for everyone. Let’s make it a space where all contributors feel welcome and proud to be involved.

Thanks for helping us make Entprep awesome! 💛
